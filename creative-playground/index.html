04 — Core Mechanics
How It Plays
🥥

Coconut Tap

Tap glowing coconut trees. Each tap shakes the tree with a satisfying bounce and drops a coin.

🎣

River Fishing

Tap rippling water spots to pull fish. Rare fish sparkle and give bonus gems.

🏡

Village Builder

Drag decorations to place them. Tap buildings to upgrade and unlock new income sources.

🌧️

Rain Bonus

When tropical rain falls, tap rain drops for double earnings. A beautiful visual event.

🛶

River Deliveries

Swipe goods onto passing boats. Each successful swipe earns bonus coins and XP.

💤

Idle Harvesting

Village earns while you're away. Return to collect a satisfying pile of rewards.

05 — Reward Systems
Feel the Love
🌅

Daily Sunrise Gift

Log in each morning to unwrap a beautiful animated gift box. Streak bonuses for consecutive days.

Harvest Bags

Idle earnings collected in woven rattan bags. Tap them open for a satisfying pop animation.

Collect cute tropical pets (sun bear, hornbill, otter). Pets auto-collect nearby coins.

Seasonal Festivals

Hari Raya, Harvest Moon, Rainy Season events with limited decorations and bonus gameplay.

🎨 Art Direction

Style: Cozy anime-inspired 2D with soft cel shading. Characters have big expressive eyes and minimal outlines.

Lighting: Warm golden hour glow dominates. Dynamic day/night cycle with soft blue moonlight evenings.

Animation: Squash-and-stretch on every tap. Leaf wobble, water ripple, bouncing coins. No sharp or jarring movement.

Weather: Tropical rain = shimmery blue droplets + pond ripples. Sunny = lens flare shimmer. Storm = dramatic purple sky.

Fonts: Rounded, bubbly display font for UI. Clean legible body text. No serif or harsh edges anywhere.

08 — UI Design Philosophy
Simple. Calm. Clear.
A

Bottom Tab Nav

Village · Fish · Shop · Pet · Profile. Five icons, always visible, never cluttered.

B

Top HUD

Coins left, gems right, XP bar center. Compact, readable at any brightness.

C

Floating Rewards

Earned coins float upward from tap point. No pop-up interruptions — rewards drift naturally.

D

Tap Feedback Ring

Every tap shows a ripple ring + sound. Instant, juicy confirmation of input.

E

One-Tap Upgrades

Tap building → upgrade menu slides up smoothly. Max 2 options visible at once.

F

No Intrusive Ads

Optional "watch for bonus" rewarded ads only. Always dismissible. Never auto-play.

09 — MVP Scope
Build This First
1

One Tap Scene

One village view: 3 coconut trees + river. Tap to collect coins. That's the whole MVP loop.

2

3 Upgradeable Buildings

Kedai (stall), Rumah (house), Pohon (tree). Each has 5 upgrade levels. Builds progression naturally.

3

Basic Idle System

Earnings accumulate offline. Return, tap bag, collect reward. Simple and satisfying.

4

Day/Night Cycle

Real-time or in-game clock shifts scene color. Morning blue → golden afternoon → dusk pink.

5

1 Starter Pet

A tiny sun bear cub that sits near the river. It reacts to taps with cute animations.

6

Daily Login Reward

Simple 7-day chest rotation. Nothing complex — just a reason to open daily.

🛠️ Recommended Engine for Solo Dev:
GDevelop (no-code, mobile export built-in, beginner-friendly) or Godot 4 (free, lightweight, excellent 2D). Target build time: 4–8 weeks for a polished MVP.
